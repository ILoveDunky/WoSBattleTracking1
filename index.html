📖 How to use RAD Tracker

RAD Tracker is a kewl tool for a semi-kewl guy.

1. Active Marches

  • Add Trackers: Input the player's name, target, and march travel time (in seconds). Enable "Pet Buff Active" if they have a buff running. Tip: Look at battle reports to see who has pet buffs active. You should also be checking to see if any march times of enemies change—get people to look at the march times of each rally lead on the enemy teams!
  • Start Countdown: You can start the countdown instantly by checking "Start countdown immediately", or manually click the Start button in the Active Marches table when the march begins.
  • Visual Cues: The rows will turn orange and pulse when a march is under 15 seconds or a buff is under 60 seconds from finishing. An audio beep will also play upon completion.

2. Rally Synchronization

There are two primary calculators for complex alliance maneuvers:

  • Same-Time Calculator: If you want 5 different players to hit a target at the exact same second, plug in their March Times and when they are currently expected to depart. The calculator will find the slowest hit time, and tell you exactly how many seconds each other player needs to delay their departure by to perfectly sync the hit.
  • Gap Calculator: If you want to see a cascading hit order. Put in everyone's data and it will tell you exactly how many seconds gap there is between the fastest player hitting and the slowest player hitting.

3. Ease of Use & Persistence

  • Auto-Save: All data, including compact view toggles and player targets, is saved to your browser automatically. It persists even if you reload the page.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Press / to focus the search bar instantly. Press S to start all unstarted timers. Press C to clear finished timers.
